Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

Zen Garden Theme: We have decided on a theme of Bauhaus. We will use the Bauhaus principle of minimalism. The Bauhaus artist believed in simplicity and effectiveness and that there is no need for additional ornamentation. This is what we are aiming for in our design of the Zen garden. We will be using linear and elegant geometric shapes and avoid any curvilinear shapes. We will also be following their use of primary colors, along with black and white. For the header text we will use a font similar to the universal typography they used and for the paragraph text we will use a san serif font.
