Join the Chamber

We would love for you to join our community. Becoming a member is simple. Just fill out the form below and you will be contacted the next bussiness day.

Membership Levels

Core Benefits

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimized) listing in the Online Chamber Business Directory on the Chamber’s website
  • Networking opportunities throughout the year with member-pricing for programs, meetings and signature events
  • Chamber referrals to your business/organization
  • Opportunity to post your company’s announcements in Member News on the Chamber website and social media platforms
  • Advertisement and sponsorship opportunities (print and online)
  • Chamber Launch orientations to explore benefits and meet other members
  • Invitations to workshops, trainings and programs designed to keep you informed about key topics, upcoming events and unique opportunities
  • Opportunity to post jobs openings on Chamber Job Board
  • Opportunity to post corporate events to Community Calendar

Non-Profit Level

  • Core Benefits plus the following
  • One registration to Chamber Launch
  • Two registrations to one Business After Hours event (must attend Chamber Launch)

Bronze Level

  • Core Benefits plus the following
  • Two registrations to Chamber Launch
  • Invitation to participate in Chamber committees
  • Enhanced website listing in Chamber Business Directory
  • Member spotlight (featured on social media platforms)
  • Legislative alerts during the legislative session

Silver Level

  • Core and bronze benefits plus the following.
  • Annual consultation with a Business Development team member to develop a customized member engagement plan
  • Hard copies of Top 10 Legislative Priorities and Legislative Scorecard

Gold Level

  • Core, bronze, and silver benefits plus the following
  • Table Sponsorship (10 seats) to the Small Business Summit (January)
  • Company advertisement on Chamber podcast (based on availability)
