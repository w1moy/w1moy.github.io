Weathered Future Site Planning Document

Name

The name for the weather website will be Weathered Future.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide up to date weather information and relevant news for each town. Each town will have their own page dedicated to their specific weather and news. There will also be a severe weather page. This page will give additional information about any warning and what a person or family should do in an emergency.

Domain Name

The domain name is avaliable and will be weatheredfuture.com
