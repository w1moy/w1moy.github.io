Meet our Team

Jackie

Jackie’s philosophy when it comes to hair is exceptionally innovative. She is a master at up to date hair trends, edgy razor sculpting and bold color. Her passion spreads from behind the chair to editorial work and runway shows. Jackies vibrant personality and artistic eye effortlessly create a bond with each guest to bring their vision to life.

Rose

Rose is a passionate, dynamic and creative artist. Her vast knowledge allows her to utilize multiple hair techniques to create progressive hair color and modern hair designs. Rose’s organic and edgy style will not only leave you feeling beautiful, but will fit your lifestyle as well.

Sam

Same is a talented, creative and experienced stylist. She specializes in advanced color techniques, balayage, and natural looking blondes. From a fashion forward style to a transformation cut and color, Sam will be happy to help you create a new you or just maintain the beautiful person you already are.
