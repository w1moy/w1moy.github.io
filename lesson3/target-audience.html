Description of Target Audience

This website is aimed at any person looking to find what the weather conditions will be on any giving day. the main audience is between the ages of 25 and 45. Most are people who have a daily commute or are traveling often. They enjoy spending timre outdoors either in the evenings or on the weekends. These people can be single or married and possible have young children. They would mostly often acess the site mobily but do have computers at home or in their workplaces.
