Description of Target Audience

This website is aimed at any person looking to find what the weather conditions will be on any giving day. the main audience is between the ages of 25 and 45. Most are people who have a daily commute or are traveling often. They enjoy spending timre outdoors either in the evenings or on the weekends. These people can be single or married and possible have young children. They would mostly often acess the site mobily but do have computers at home or in their workplaces.

Scenarios

What will the weather be like right now?

A person will go to the website and enter thier zip code. From thier they will see that it is raining and they need to take a rain coat.

What will the weather be this weekend?

A family is looking to go to an outdoor festive this comeing weekend and need to know what clothing to wear and pack. they found that it will be a sunny weekend and they they need to take sunscreen and hats.

Will there be snow this week?

A nieghbor was told that there was going to be snow this week. So they come to the website to see if that is in fact true and if so what day. the nieghbor is correct and it will be snowing at the end of the week.

Will there be a chance for lighting?

A person is planning hike in the Rocky Mountian National Forest and is wondering what the chances of lighting are. They find that this is a small chance of lightning in the afternoon so plan to take their hike in the morning.

What will the weather be in the mountains?

A group of friends is planning to go camping in the moutians and need to know what the weather will be. They find that it is a mix of sun and rain.
