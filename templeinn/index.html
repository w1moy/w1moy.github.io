The Salt Lake City temple is in the center of down town Salt Lake. It is surrounded by beautiful gardens that are tended year around. At Christmas time each year the temple grounds are covered in lights and people come from all over to view the magnificant site, even in the bitter cold. The temple was annouced July 28, 1847 but building didn't start until February 14, 1853 and it wasn't dedicated until April 6, 1893. They will be starting a renevation in January of 2020.
